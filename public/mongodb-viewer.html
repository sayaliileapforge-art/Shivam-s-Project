🗄️ MongoDB Database Structure

Database: aaryaleap (or your database name)
Collections:
  ├── projects
  │   ├── _id (ObjectId - auto generated)
  │   ├── name (string)
  │   ├── description (string)
  │   ├── clientId (ObjectId - reference to client)
  │   ├── status (string: draft, active, archived, completed)
  │   └── createdAt (Date)
  │
  ├── clients
  │   ├── _id (ObjectId - auto generated)
  │   ├── name (string)
  │   ├── email (string - unique)
  │   ├── phone (string)
  │   ├── address (string)
  │   ├── city (string)
  │   ├── country (string)
  │   ├── status (string: active, inactive, blocked)
  │   └── createdAt (Date)
  │
  └── products
      ├── _id (ObjectId - auto generated)
      ├── name (string)
      ├── sku (string - unique)
      ├── category (string)
      ├── price (number)
      ├── stock (number)
      ├── visibility (string: public, private, restricted)
      └── createdAt (Date)
